# Art direction

## Core look

- Pure black, white, and grayscale only
- Hand-drawn ink with charcoal or graphite texture
- Medieval environments carry the composition
- Human figures remain small, obscured, back-facing, or silhouetted
- Cinematic portrait-friendly framing with readable negative space
- Weather, architecture, objects, and damage communicate history

## Avoid

- Hero portraits or face-dominant framing
- Colour accents
- Glossy digital-painting surfaces
- Embedded dialogue, captions, buttons, or logos
- Direct imitation of a living artist or another franchise
- Modern clothing, weapons, or architecture

## File convention

Use one image per story node:

`assets/artwork/ch01_<node-id>.png`

Recommended working size: 1440 × 1000 pixels. Keep essential detail away from the outer 5% of the frame. Source files may be stored separately; only optimized game-ready exports belong in the runtime folder.
